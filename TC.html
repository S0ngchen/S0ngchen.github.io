Tropical cyclones Forecast

Why TC

My interest in tropical cyclones (TCs) began when Super Typhoon Mangkhut  struck my hometown. The flooding that poured into buildings and the broken trees scattered everywhere left a deep impression on me. From that moment, I became captivated by this devastating yet fascinating natural disaster. I aspire to gain a deeper understanding of it to help mitigate its impact on society.

Improving TC forecast

I conducted this project to enhance the accuracy of TC intensity forecasting by the China Meteorological Administration (CMA). The model is based on Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks and combines FNL forecast data with real-time CMA TC data, enabling a significant reduction in forecasting errors. Codes can be found in GitHub repository (after paper published).
